03 — Me

About Anushriya

I'm Anushriya Roy — originally from New Delhi, India, now based in London completing my MSc in Analytics and Management at London Business School. My BTech in Computer Science gave me the technical foundations; LBS is where I'm learning to point them at business problems that matter.

My work sits across operations research (integer programming, advertising optimisation), data engineering (multi-source ETL, API integration, relational schema design), and AI engineering (LLM applications, semantic search, automated prompt optimisation). I'm most energised when these disciplines intersect.

One area I keep coming back to is fashion tech — an industry that generates enormous data but is still surprisingly underserved by modern analytics. From demand forecasting and sustainability measurement to personalised discovery, I think there's a huge opportunity to build tools that are both technically interesting and genuinely useful to people.

Outside of work: tennis, books, good food, and thinking about how sustainable systems get built and measured. I care about doing things properly. ☕
